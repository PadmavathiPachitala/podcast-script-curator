[1:00 — ARCHITECTURE]
(show assets/architecture_diagram.png)
Let's walk through how this works under the hood. 
(pause)
First, the workflow starts when a user inputs tech topics. 
The input goes straight into our Security Checkpoint node. This node scrubs personal info like emails and phone numbers, checks for prompt injection, and logs every audit step in JSON format.
If it is safe, the workflow routes to the News Research node.
Here, our Orchestrator agent delegates research tasks to our news_researcher sub-agent.
The news_researcher sub-agent calls our local Model Context Protocol, or MCP server, to fetch matching article headlines and summaries.
Next, we hit the Human Review node. The workflow pauses here, asking the user to review the research summaries and give their sign-off.
Once the user types 'yes', the workflow routes to the Generate Script node.
The Orchestrator agent delegates dialogue drafting to our script_writer sub-agent.
The script_writer sub-agent generates a conversational dialogue script between Host A and Host B, and automatically saves it to our local workspace.
Finally, the workflow ends at the Final Output node, printing the completed script.
